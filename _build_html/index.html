Get Started¶

  1. Navigate to the CyVerse User Portal

    • If you do not have a CyVerse account, go here for instructions on how to create one: Create a CyVerse Account

  2. In the upper right corner of the page, click the ACCOUNT button

  3. Scroll down to find the Password section

  4. If you have forgotten your password, click the link to reset your password.

    • You can also find the link here: Forgot Your CyVerse Password

  5. If you know your old password, enter it in the first box of the Password section

  6. Choose a new password, and enter it into the New Password and Confirm New Password boxes

  7. Click the UPDATE button to update your password
