*Get Started*
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. 	#### Comment: Step title should be descriptive (i.e. Cleaning Read data) ###


1. Navigate to the |Cyverse User Portal|

   - If you do not have a CyVerse account, go here for instructions on how to create one: |Cyverse Account Creation| 

2. In the upper right corner of the page, click the :guilabel:`&ACCOUNT` button

3. Scroll down to find the **Password** section

4. If you have forgotten your password, click the link to reset your password. 

   - You can also find the link here: |CyVerse Forgot Password|

5. If you know your old password, enter it in the first box of the **Password** section

6. Choose a new password, and enter it into the *New Password* and *Confirm New Password* boxes

7. Click the :guilabel:`&UPDATE` button to update your password
